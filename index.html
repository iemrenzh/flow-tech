FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer emergency AC repair?

Yes — we offer 24/7 emergency AC repair across Miami-Dade County. Call us anytime at (305) 264-5051 and we'll dispatch a technician as quickly as possible.

What brands do you service?

We service all major AC and HVAC brands including Carrier, Lennox, Trane, Rheem, Goodman, and more. Our technicians are trained on the latest high-efficiency systems.

How do I know if I need a repair or a full replacement?

Our technicians give you an honest, unbiased recommendation based on the age of your system, repair costs, and energy efficiency. We never push replacements unnecessarily.

Are your prices upfront?

Always. We provide a full quote before any work begins. No surprise charges, no fine print. What we quote is what you pay.

Is Flow-Tech licensed and insured?

Yes. Flow-Tech is fully licensed, insured, and BBB Accredited. You're in safe, professional hands from start to finish.
